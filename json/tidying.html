Why are the commas there?

We are displaying the [arrays] as "strings", the commas that separate each value are being treated as part of the [arrays], and are thus being displayed at the top of the table.

We are going to use the .replace() method with two arguments to remove these commas. The first argument is to look for all instances of the commas, while the second is to replace them. We can simply append this method to the end of our code:

"" is the second argument, and means simply an empty "string".
